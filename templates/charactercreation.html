Add In Items To Your Inventory

It is important to note that all of the following categories come with a designated spot to insert a name along with additional descriptions of the items
Note that items come in groups of the following:
Container-> An object that hold water, important for implimentation of water tracking
Siege Equipment -> Has AC, Damage Immunities, and Hitpoints
Poison -> Has a type and cost
Adventuring Gear -> Has cost and weight
Weapon -> Has a weight, cost, and damage
Armor & Shield -> has an equipped status, weight, cost, and AC
Explosive -> Has a cost and weight
Tools -> Have a cost and weight
Trinket -> nothing special
Firearm -> Have cost, damage, and weight
Other -> The broad category of anything that doesn't fit the previous, has a cost and weight
Wondrous -> nothing special
Ration -> important for the implementation of the rationing tracker, has nothing special
Spells -> Yes.. Spells! These are not technically items, but you will add any spells you have here! Spells have a duration, components, level, range, and casting time.
